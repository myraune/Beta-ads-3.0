Frequently asked questions

How does Beta Ads proof of delivery work?

Every ad_completed event is recorded in UTC and contributes to campaign impressions and payout calculations.

Why are overlays not showing during my stream?

Check OBS source visibility, verify heartbeat connection, and confirm your setup status in the setup route.

When can I request payout?

Payout unlocks after threshold is met and campaign review is complete. Admin can mark payout status manually in MVP.
